About NiloFibreNet

Pioneering Digital Transformation in Bhutan

Our Story

Founded in the heart of Bhutan, NiloFibreNet was born from a vision to bridge the digital divide and bring world-class internet connectivity to every corner of the Kingdom of Bhutan.

In a country known for measuring Gross National Happiness, we believe that reliable internet access is essential for education, healthcare, business, and staying connected with loved ones. Our mission is to empower every Bhutanese citizen with the tools they need to thrive in the digital age.

Our Mission

To provide fast, reliable, and affordable internet services that connect communities across Bhutan, fostering economic growth, education, and cultural preservation while respecting our nation's unique values and environment.

Our Vision

To be Bhutan's leading internet service provider, recognized for exceptional service quality, innovative solutions, and our commitment to sustainable development in harmony with Bhutan's environmental and cultural heritage.
